Honors and Award

>During my leisure time, I liked to take part in some academic contests with my team partners, especially mathematics contests and robot contest.

Of course, I love advanced and cool technologies. From small softwares to amazing hoverboards, anything can be charming. If you have any interesting things to share, just contact me, I'll be really grateful!

"Robcup" China Robot Contest

October 28-30, 2016 China robot contest was held in Changsha, Hunan. As the leader of the Undergraduate Innovative Test Program,I participate in contest on behalf of my team, and won the championship.

This is the first time for our team to win the championship, so it has a significance to us and our school. The result of the project can be used as the referee system of the next simulation project competition, and also has the teaching reference function to other students in the future.

Mathematical Contest in Modelling

My two teammates and I continuted working for three days to solve a Mathematical modeling problem and write a paper for it.

Trough our joint effort, we won National 1st Prize in 2016 China Undergraduate Mathematical Contest in Modelling，and Meritorious Winnerin 2016 Mathematical Contest in Modeling (MCM).
