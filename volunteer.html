Volunteering Experience

I was awarded the honorary title of outstanding volunteers in our school.

"Public welfare" has become a keyword in my life. Since entering the university, I have organized and participated in volunteer activities more than 40 times, spending more than 200 hours. I took part in Shanghai marathon, Shanghai half marathon , compusory tutor, Sunflower education program, school library and many other important volunteer activities.

School outstanding volunteers

It's the time when I was awarded the honorary title of outstanding volunteers. I was extremely exciting, delighted, and also grateful.

As a college student,I sincerely hope that everyone would join the volunteering work.It is not only good to our society, but also a chance for us to grow and to learn.

Siyuan Welfare Summer Teacher Support Program

In my summer vacation in 2015, I went to the west region of China, Tengchong, Yunnan, to relieve the shortage of teachers there. We did teacher support there for about one month.

Athough life there is very tough and difficult, it's a memorable and moving volunteering experience.

Shanghai Marathon volunteers

Shanghai International Marathon is one of Shanghai's major sporting events and one of Shanghai's urban landscape sports, nearly 2,400 event volunteers to undertake the course of drinking water, garbage collection, prizes and other work.

I was in the starting point volunteers group. We started our work at two o'clock in the morning. We collected items for the participants, recycled the garbage on the site, kept the road clean, created the best game environment for them, and applaud to each participant.

SJTU 120th anniversary celebration volunteers

On April 8, 2016, the 120th anniversary of the National Chiao Tung University began to celebrate. I served as the school volunteer team leader, arrived early at the School gymnasium.

I greeted the 96th alumni, led them to watch the celebration of the opening ceremony, and them guided them to go around the school. Chatting with the old alumni, I learned some interesting history of our school, and made friends with them.
